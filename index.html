Frequently Asked Questions

1.How To Get Started With MEEDA?

1. Decide which platform to use (Twitter/Facebook/WhatsApp)
2. Send hi or hello to MEEDA
3. You'll ask to create account if it's your first time
4. After that, credit your wallet
5. Enjoy using MEEDA by selecting option that suits your need.

2. How Do I Check My Ballance?

• Reply with 1 to check your wallet ballance
• Dial *461*4# to check MTN Data ballance
• Dial *140# to check Airtel Data ballance
• Dial *127*0# to check Glo Data ballance
• Dial *229*9# to check 9mobile(SME) Data ballance
• Dial *228# to check 9mobile(Gifting) Data ballance

3.How Do I Fund My Wallet?

• After creating your account with MEEDA
• A unique account number will be created for you
• Make a deposit to the account whenever you want to fund your wallet
• And your wallet will be credited automatically.
